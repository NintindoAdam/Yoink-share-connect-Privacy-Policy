Yoink Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026

Yoink does not collect your data. Your data is stored on your device and synced to your own private iCloud when enabled, and sharing happens only when you choose to share directly with the person in front of you.

What Yoink stores

Yoink is a digital business card app. The card you build, the contacts you receive, and your app settings are stored on your device and synced to your own private iCloud (CloudKit private database) under your Apple ID. We do not operate a server for this data, and we cannot access it.

Received cards are saved inside Yoink and are not written to your system Contacts app. If you allow location access, Yoink may save an optional coarse meet-location (about 100m precision) with a received card.

What we collect

Nothing. Yoink has no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. We do not run a developer-operated backend, we do not receive your card data, and we cannot see how you use the app.

Sign in with Apple

Yoink uses Sign in with Apple to create a local account identifier on your device. This identifier is stored on-device only, is not synced to iCloud by Yoink, and is never sent to any developer-operated server.

Sharing a card

When you share your card, it travels directly between the two devices — by scanning an offline QR code with the camera, or through an encrypted nearby peer-to-peer connection. Your profile photo is shared through that same direct encrypted transfer. Nothing is routed through a developer-operated server.

Opening links

Your card can include links to your social media or website. When you or someone else taps one of those links, iOS opens it in the relevant app or browser. That action is handled by Apple and the destination service, under their own privacy terms, not ours.

Deleting your data

You are always in control. From the app's settings you can:

  • Delete your account, which removes your local account identifier.
  • Delete your card and any contacts you've saved.

Deleting your account or card removes data from local device storage and also removes Yoink's synced copy from your private iCloud. Deleting the app removes local Yoink data from the device.

Children

Yoink is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, regardless of age.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time as Yoink evolves. When we do, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
